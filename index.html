Tech.Care

LTH Interaction Design, Basic Course

The project was developed in collaboration with two other students in the course Interaction Design, Basic Course. The task was to design a smart home device which would facilitate human needs. The Rescue of Dataville

LTH Interaction 1: Neuro Modelling, Cognitive Robotics and Agents
Interaction 2: Virtuality and Cognitive Modelling

The Rescue of Dataville is a learning game which I and my team have developed to improve knowledge about data literacy for children in ages ten to twelve. It was a collaboration between master engineer students and cognitive science master students. With the Swedish National Curriculum as a starting point, using theories in experience design, educational technology, and cognitive science, we created an educational game revolving around several mini-games, set in the fictional world of Dataville. I was the project leader and responsible for the development of the prototype and areas concerning interaction design. The project was performed during two courses: Interaction 1: Neuro Modelling, Cognitive Robotics and Agents and Interaction 2: Virtuality and Cognitive Modelling.
